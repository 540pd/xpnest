Study in Europe Counselling

Choosing to study abroad is a once in a lifetime decision for many, and you need to consult with someone who truly cares. While there may be hundreds of universities, what matters to you is that one university and a course that is the best for you.

You need to think long-term, envision the future and make an informed decision. Our expert counselors will guide you through every step, helping you select the right university and program that aligns with your career goals and personal aspirations.

Other Services

Genuine Guidance

Our team is led by seasoned professionals with prestigious academic backgrounds, ensuring you receive guidance from some of the finest experts in the field. We prioritize your needs and focus on finding the perfect academic match for your future.

Application Processing

Having been in the industry for almost two decades, we know the nuances of filling applications with universities. Our expert team will help you prepare compelling documents, personal statements, and ensure all requirements are met for a successful application.

Financial Guidance

Get your financial documents in order (crucial for the visa), arrange education loans and forex, through experts with 10+ years of experience. We'll help you understand the costs involved and plan your finances effectively for your study abroad journey.

Visa Processing

With a 99% visa success rate and having received multiple industry awards, you can trust that your visa documentation is in safe hands. Our meticulous approach ensures that your visa application meets all requirements for a smooth approval process.

Pre-Departure Counselling

Traveling abroad alone for studies can be overwhelming. However, when you are given the right guidance and have a network of friends, it will be fun. We provide comprehensive pre-departure sessions covering everything you need to know before leaving.

Post-Landing Support

Whether it's navigating immigration services like UKBA or Garda in Ireland, or finding suitable accommodation, we ensure you know exactly what to do and how to do it. Our support continues even after you've landed in your study destination.
