Our Journey

May 2004

Eureka Moment

Started our journey as an IELTS training institute with 2 offices. This was the year with a lot of learning and finding our footing in the market.

Feb 2005

Rapid Growth

Opened our third IELTS training office in Noida. This was a period of strategic expansion and building our presence in key educational hubs.

April 2006

Beginning of an Era

We realized that there was an opportunity to build a market for higher education in Ireland. It had all the perfect conditions for Indian students to study, build their careers and live a peaceful life with a higher standard of living.

April 2007

Baby Steps Into The UK

UK was an established market with hundreds of study abroad consultants. Defying all odds, we still took that plunge to make it big in the UK.

Nov 2010

Nov 2016

The Award Saga Begins

Nov 2019

Market Leaders

2021-Present
